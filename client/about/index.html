Welcome to Loopin!

Loopin was created with one mission in mind - to simplify group event planning. Whether you're organising a party, a weekend away, or a family reunion, planning events as a group should be fun, easy not stressful. Loopin is here to help keep everyone in the loop and ensure everything is handled smoothly.

Our Purpose

We know that organising an event involves a lot of moving parts, and when multiple people are involved, it can be challenging to keep everyone on the same page. Loopin was built to bridge this gap by offering a simple, intuitive platform for event planners and participants. With Loopin, members can create and assign tasks, track what’s done, and monitor what still needs doing in one convenient place.

Why Choose Loopin?

Our platform allows groups to:

  • Streamline event planning through organised task lists
  • Assign tasks to specific members, so nothing falls through the cracks
  • Track event progress and see what’s completed at a glance
  • Communicate easily with our chat page

Loopin is designed to take the stress out of planning, so you and your group can focus on enjoying your time together.

Join Us on This Journey

We are excited to be part of your special events, providing a tool that keeps everyone aligned and organised. Welcome to Loopin — let's plan together!
